The Long Trip Home

Created By:Arely, Ashante, and Katelyn



You don't test your luck and you call your mom who picks you up and immediately starts yelling at you. She tells you that you're grounded because, "you were supposed to take the bus," but you didn't. You try to explain to her what was going on but she doesn't want to hear it. She sends you to your room and tells you to not come out until she calls you.
