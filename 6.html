The Long Trip Home

Created By:Arely, Ashante, and Katelyn



As you take the scenic route, home you stumble upon your friends from middle school. You went on to do good in school while they fell by the wayside. You still talk to each other in passing, but you haven't hung out in a while. Their hangouts aren't legal, but you're almost positive that you can lead them in the right direction with one hangout. You can either hang out with them, or walk passed them because you need to maintain your scholarly reputation.


What do you do?
